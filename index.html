About Me

¿Who am I?

I´m a person, a girl, a student, a daughter, and most importantly a Gemini. My costumes are from Oaxaca, my beliefs from what I´ve learned during the past 20 years. Not an animal person even though I admire most of them. In my next life, I pray to come back as an octopus. For now, nothing makes me happier than a casual firefly or hummingbird. Movies are my hobby and stories my passion. I create one in my head every hour of the day. Sometimes I´m the queen, others an Italian gangster, who knows. One thing is for sure and that´s that I´m always have fun.
